Magic

The Weave of Aethrya

In the wake of the Veil Accord, magic has become more volatile yet more precious. Academic study in Leilon and raw practice in the wilds have categorized magic into distinct tiers of mastery.

Tiers of Spells

  • Cantrips: Minor effects possible through rote practice.
  • Novice: Basic combat and utility spells.
  • Adept: Intermediate magic used by seasoned practitioners.
  • Expert: Advanced spells capable of shifting the tides of battle.
  • Master: The apex of mortal magic, rare and terrifying.

Rituals

Magic that takes longer to cast but does not expend the caster's immediate reserves. Often used for regional effects or deep divination.

Curses

Dark magic that lingers, often requiring specialized healing or divine intervention to break. Curses are a significant risk for those dabbling in forbidden lore.
