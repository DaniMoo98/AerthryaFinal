Barbarian

Typical barbarians harbor a fury that dwells within. Their rage grants them superior strength, durability, and speed. Barbarians who follow the Path of the Storm Herald learn instead to transform their rage into a mantle of primal magic that swirls around them.

Core Features

Rage

Taking a bonus action in combat you can fly into a primal rage for 10 rounds. You gain +1 to Strength, saving throw bonuses, and resistance to physical damage while unarmored or in light/medium armor.

Unarmored Defense

Your AC is 10 + Dexterity + Constitution when not wearing armor.

Primal Paths

Path of the Totem

A spiritual journey where the Barbarian accepts a spirit animal as guide. Totems like Bear, Wolf, and Eagle grant specific boons during rage.

Path of the Zealot

Deities inspire these warriors to channel divine power into their battle fury, dealing radiant damage and inspiring allies.
