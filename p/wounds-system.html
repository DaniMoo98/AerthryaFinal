Wounds System

The Reality of Injury

Aethrya utilizes a Wounds system to simulate the lingering effects of combat. HP represents immediate stamina, while Wounds represent actual trauma.

The Four Levels of Wounds

LevelEffectsRecovery Period
Lesser-1 Dexterity1 Hour
Medium-2 Dex, -1 Str, -1 Con2 Hours
Serious-3 Dex, -2 Str, -2 Con, -1 Int/Wis, -10 Init3 Hours
Devastating-4 Dex, -4 Str, -4 Con, -2 Int/Wis, -20 Init4 Hours

Methods of Healing

  • Natural Healing: 1d4+CON roll every 24 hours.
  • First Aid: Minor treatment for lesser wounds (Civilians).
  • Surgery: 1d15+Medicine roll, requires Anatomist feat or high skill.
  • Magical Healing: 20d2 roll, available to Clerics and Druids (6 Spell Points).

Note: All wounds inflict a "Recently Injured" status. Healing cannot be attempted while this status is active.
